hi, i'm mike ferguson

Born and raised in a small town, I’ve always been drawn to the way people interact with their surroundings. Growing up in a close-knit community shaped my approach to design—focusing on accessibility, clarity, and intuitive experiences that bring people together. Working at the only Tim Hortons in town taught me the value of collaboration, adaptability, and problem-solving in a fast-paced environment. These skills translate directly into my work in design, where teamwork and user-centered thinking are essential. I bring my designs to life using tools like Figma, HTML, Squarespace, and Webflow, ensuring both aesthetics and functionality work hand in hand. Whether prototyping interfaces or building web experiences, I strive to create seamless, engaging designs. Outside of design, my hobbies include anything Pokémon, playing video games, and creating my own video games when my hyper-niche idea for a game doesn't exist yet! Whether it be about design, opportunities, or any hobbies we may have in common, shoot me a message! I'd love to chat :)
